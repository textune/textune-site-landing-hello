What is Textune?

Textune.io helps you understand more — by reading less. We turn messy communication into structured insight using AI: classify content, detect tone and intent, tag what matters, and generate instant TL;DR. Sync or async. One API. From chaos to clarity — instantly.

Smart Classification

Group messages by type, intent or domain. Using AI models, Textune can intelligently categorize messages and transcripts into actionable groups.

Tagging & Keywords

Extract meaningful tags, topics or identifiers to help automate routing, filtering or decision-making processes.

TL;DR Summaries

Automatically condense long messages into short, meaningful summaries for faster understanding and reporting.

Tone & Intent Detection

Detect emotional tone and intention of communication to support classification, alerting or automation flows.

Clean HTML & Quotes

Automatically remove signatures, previous replies, quote blocks and HTML noise for clean processing.
