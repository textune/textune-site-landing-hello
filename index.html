What is Textune?

Textune.io helps you understand more — by reading less. We transform emails, messages and documents into actionable data with AI-powered classification, tone and intent detection, tagging, and TL;DR summaries. Sync or async. One API. Instant clarity from unstructured text.

Smart Classification

Automatically categorize incoming messages, emails or tickets based on topic, intent, or business context. Identify issues such as complaints, leads, opt-outs, or urgent requests — ready to power automation flows.

Tagging & Keywords

Extract business-relevant keywords, named entities and internal labels from any unstructured content. Improve filtering, searching and routing of messages in CRM, helpdesk or internal tools.

TL;DR Summaries

Instantly summarize lengthy emails, transcripts or notes into short, digestible blurbs. Save time and improve clarity for agents and end-users.

Tone & Intent Detection

Detect the emotional tone (e.g. frustration, politeness, sarcasm) and communication intent (e.g. request, question, threat). React faster and smarter to customer needs and urgency.

Clean HTML & Quotes

Remove boilerplate, email signatures, quote blocks, reply chains and HTML clutter. Prepare raw messages for analysis, training, and downstream processing.

How it works

Textune is an API-first platform that turns messy communication into structured insight — with built-in functions like cleanup, extraction, tone and intent detection, classification and summarization.
